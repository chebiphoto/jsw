We are based in Shikoku (Kagawa Prefecture) and regularly shoot in Kyoto, Setouchi and Tokyo. Each location offers something distinct — from Kyoto's ancient temple gardens to the quiet coastal light of Setouchi. Travel to other locations in Japan can be arranged on request.

Yes. Our team communicates fluently in both English and Japanese. From your first inquiry through to your final gallery delivery, every interaction is in English. We handle all venue and vendor communication in Japanese on your behalf.

Our full-service Japan elopement packages include: dedicated local planner, wedding photography, videography, ceremony officiant, hair and makeup, kimono or dress styling, venue sourcing, and legal marriage coordination. Photography-only and pre-wedding shoot packages are also available. Contact us for a custom quote.

Yes. Foreigners can legally marry in Japan. The process requires documentation from your home country's embassy and submission at the local municipal office. We provide full guidance and coordination throughout the process, so you don't need to navigate Japanese bureaucracy alone.

Japan is beautiful year-round. Cherry blossom season (late March–April) is the most sought-after for Kyoto. Autumn (November–December) brings stunning foliage across Shikoku and Kyoto. Spring and autumn are ideal for Setouchi's soft coastal light. We recommend booking at least 3–6 months in advance for popular seasons.
